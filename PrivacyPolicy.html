Karuah Chess - Privacy Policy

The Karuah Chess application does not collect or store personal information, except in the following circumstances;

  • If using voice commands on Karuah Chess for Android, the application uses the Android Speech Recognition service to convert spoken words in to commands that interact with the application. Your voice data is processed by this Google provided speech service. To find out more about how Google handles your data, refer to their privacy information at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • If using voice commands on Karuah Chess for Windows, the application uses the Windows Media Speech Recognition service to convert spoken words in to commands that interact with the application. Your voice data is processed by this Microsoft provided speech service. To find out more about how Microsoft handles your data, refer to their privacy information at https://privacy.microsoft.com/
